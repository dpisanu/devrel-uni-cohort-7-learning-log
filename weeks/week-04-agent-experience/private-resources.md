## Public-safe summary of private material

The private session and activity material informed the following public-safe themes:

* DevRel can span community, content and product / developer experience work.
* Agent Experience asks whether agent-assisted developer workflows can understand and use a product.
* Documentation, SDKs, CLIs and examples may need to become more machine-consumable.
* PlainTrack should keep its deterministic core while allowing agents to assist around the workflow.
* The Miro activity asked participants to inspect where agents could interact with their projects, what currently breaks and what they would redesign.
