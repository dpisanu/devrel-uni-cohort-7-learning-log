# Week 02: Navigating Your Tech Career in an Industry That Won’t Slow Down

## Overview

**Week:** Week 02  
**Theme:** Tech career direction in a fast-moving industry  
**Session:** Navigating Your Tech Career in an Industry That Won’t Slow Down  
**Speaker:** Nader Dabit  
**Project framing:** Open Source Readiness Field Guide  
**Status:** Research mapping and scope reduction

Week 02 focused on career adaptability, visible learning, timing, experimentation and creating more optionality in a changing technology landscape.

For me, the session landed in a complicated way. It was energizing and encouraging, especially the framing of a broader technologist identity instead of a narrow role identity. At the same time, it created pressure: more tools, more signals, more possible paths and more rabbit holes competing with the actual project work.

The project stayed under the umbrella of the **Open Source Readiness Field Guide**, but Week 02 narrowed the immediate focus toward **open source enablement research and material curation**. Instead of trying to create a polished guide immediately, the first tangible artifact became a rough research map and source inventory.
