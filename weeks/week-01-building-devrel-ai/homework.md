## Objective

This week was about grounding the cohort journey by defining what I am building and reflecting on my role as a builder in an AI-shaped world.

This file documents the homework structure. My actual answer is in [`my-submission.md`](./my-submission.md).

## Part 1: Project selection

Choose a project to develop during the cohort. The project should be realistic enough to iterate on during the five-week program.

Potential project types included:

- Educational content series
- Documentation revamp or onboarding guide
- Sample app or demo project
- Community initiative
- Workshop or talk
- Developer-focused content strategy
- AI-assisted DevRel tool

### Project framing questions

The project description should answer:

- What am I building?
- Who is it for?
- What problem does it solve?
- Why does this project matter to me personally?
- What actions can I take to make sure I succeed in finishing the project?
- What would success look like by the end of the cohort?

## Part 2: Reflection

Answer the following prompts:

### Human Edge

What skills am I relying on that I believe AI cannot replace?

### AI and uncertainty

What is one fear I have about AI changing my field?

### Unlearning and learning

- What is something I had to unlearn because of AI?
- What is something new I have learned because of AI?

## Part 3: Build in public

Create a social media post sharing:

- That I have been challenged to build something in public for DevRel University Cohort 7
- What I am building
- Why I am excited or nervous about it
- Optional: what support I might need for the project

The post should be authentic rather than overly polished.

## Submission format

Submit:

- Written assignment in Google Docs or Markdown
- Link to the social media post

## What the assignment is looking for

- Willingness to try
- Personal motivation and authenticity
- Critical thinking about how AI shapes how we learn, teach, and build
- Willingness to share the journey publicly
